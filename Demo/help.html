Need Help?

This page will help you resolve login issues.

Forgot Your Password?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page to reset your password.

Trouble Logging In?

  • Ensure you selected the correct Role (Student or Staff).
  • Check that your ID or Username is correct.
  • Passwords are case-sensitive — check for typos!

Technical Troubleshooting

  • Use an updated browser (Chrome(recommended), Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Try logging in with Incognito or Private mode.
